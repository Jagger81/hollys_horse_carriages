What's The Story?

I have been providing a quality service to Dublin and its surrounding areas since July 2018. Originally founded in the Dublin 8 neighbourhood and in the heart of The Liberities near the Guinness Store House. I have recently relocated to the lovely area of Ladytown, Co Kildare.


I'm here to Serve You!

Whether it's a birthday party, wedding, or other important family event you can rely on me to ensure that you have a thoroughly enjoyable experience.
Myself and Chico will be immaculately turned out (presented) at all times.

Services Offered



Birthdays

Is your loved one a horse lover? Or perhaps a romantic?

Why not surprise them with a trip to remember on their birthday. A perfect way to spend a special night. Available as a surprise and complementary refreshments are given.


Weddings

Feel like a queen on your Big Day! Arrive in style in my luxurious original Landau to the sound of hoofbeats to wow your guests.

Plumes and bows of you colour choice available.


Communion

Give your little one the royal treatment and make them feel like a prince or princess, with a horse-drawn carriage for their Big Day.

Available as a surprise. Plumes and bows of you colour choice available.


Confirmation

For the horse mad pre-teen to give them prince/princess worthy transport.

Plumes and bows of you colour choice available.


Carriage Tours

Ever wanted a unique way to view our beautiful surroundings in a calm way?

Book today and be sure to enjoy a half hour or hour long tour through a special location of your choice.



Proposals

A special, romantic, "go all out" way to pop the big question.

Complementary bubbly to sip away at, to ease the pre-question jitter. Plumes and bows of you colour choice available.


Valentine's Day

The most romantic day of the year!

Why not enjoy some couple time with a nice relaxing carriage ride, while sipping on champagne.


Christmas Trips

Short trip around Newbridge to get into that Christmas spirit.

See the lights in a traditional, leisurely manner. Time for photographs afterwards.

This trip is done in pre-booked slots only. No showing up on the day and hoping for the best. Deposits secure your booking.

Trips commence and terminate at McDonnell's pub.

Added Extras
