Meet The Team

Holly

My name is Holly Ward. I have loved horses all my life. I first started driving just over 3 years ago and I immediatley fell in love with it.

I got my licence 3 years ago and made history by becoming the first and only licenced and insured female carriage driver in Dublin City.

I took part in the carriage driving section in the Tullow Show for the last 2 years running and took home a 3rd and 2nd place in the shows respectively.

My business is just starting out but I have my sights set on much larger things in a couple of years to come.

Chico

Chico is a 5 year old cross breed. He has a witty personality and is known for his character. He enjoys a carrot or two (although he's partial to a chicken nugget or two), then likes to be left alone.

He is a hard worker and is enjoying getting to look stunning in many different coloured plumes, although I think pink suits him very well.

Chico will be your trusty steed for your occassion. He looks forward to meeting you and helping to make your day or night the best it can be.

The Carriage

This beautiful original 1900's landau is the perfect addition to any day. Whether it be the biggest day of your life or just a simple tour around Dublin City it suits every occassion down to the ground.

The hood can be fully closed, fully opened, half up or half down. It is complete with windows for those unsightly rainy days we get here in Ireland.

It is a comfortable carriage in beautiful condition. It has striking red and black colouring and will be perfect for all occasions.
